Typography

Headings

Heading 1

Won't be used in text, there will be a logo in SVG

Heading 2

Heading 2 aka <h2> is used as a post title

Heading 3

Heading 3 aka <h3> is used as title for the most important sections of a post

Heading 4

Heading 4 aka <h3> serves as a secondary title for important sections

Heading 5

Heading 5 aka <h5> may be used when there is a need for important sections

Heading 6

Heading 6 aka <h6> will be used only in case of complete and utter desperation or finer sectioning

Text

Basics

Italics in <i>and emphasized text in <em>

Bold in <b> and bold in <strong>

Underline in <u>

u, i and b shouldn't be used at all. em, strong, small and others only when it makes semantically sense. Also this is an example of <small>
